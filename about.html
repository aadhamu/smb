Founded in (establishment year), SMB Designs began as a humble creative studio with a focus on quality, detail, and timeless design. What started as a simple idea soon evolved into a respected name in the world of premium craftsmanship.

Our Materials

At SMB Designs, we take pride in using only the finest locally sourced materials. Based in Kaduna, Nigeria, all of our products are carefully crafted in-house by skilled artisans dedicated to quality and detail.

From material selection to the final stitch, every step is handled with care. We believe that great design starts with great materials and even greater craftsmanship.
